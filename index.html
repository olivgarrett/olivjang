Hello! My name is Olivia Garrett.

I’m a student at the University of Missouri working on a dual-degree in investigative journalism and political science with a minor in Spanish.

I’m currently reporting on community news at the Columbia Missourian and fact-checking for PolitiFact Missouri.

Previously, I was an intern at POLITICO Europe in Brussels, Belgium. There, I primarily worked on the production desk, crafting social media posts and designing pages for the print edition. Before that, I was an education beat reporter and a member of the community outreach team at the Missourian. I was also a beat reporter and editor on the university news section at the Maneater, MU’s student newspaper.

Writing is my main passion, but I also enjoy photography and design. I coded this portfolio website.

I’m from Texas and I’m a fan of BBQ, Tex-Mex and kolaches. When I’m not writing, I like to bake and explore new places.

I'm planning on graduating in December 2019. After graduation, I hope to work as a reporter for a print or digital news organization.
