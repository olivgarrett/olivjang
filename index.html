I'm a reporter with an interest in local journalism. I currently live in Fulton, Missouri, where I cover everything and anything for the Fulton Sun. My beat includes the universities, schools and rural communities of Callaway County, including Holts Summit, New Bloomfield and Mokane. During the COVID-19 pandemic, I've written about school closures and reopenings, the efforts of local governments and the reactions of businesses, faith communities and families.

In December 2019, I graduated from the University of Missouri with a dual-degree in investigative journalism and political science and a minor in Spanish.

While in school, I spent much of my time in the Columbia Missourian newsroom, with stints as an assistant city editor, reporter, graphic designer and member of the community outreach team. Along the way, I also worked with PolitiFact Missouri, POLITICO Europe and my campus newspaper, The Maneater.

I built this portfolio website using HTML, CSS and a bit of JavaScript.
