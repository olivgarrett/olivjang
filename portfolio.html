Design

While an intern at POLITICO Europe, I designed several pages each week for the print edition using Adobe InDesign. This entailed picking out visuals and then laying out the headlines, bylines, photos, photo captions and articles to fit the constraints of the page in an appealing manner. Outside of internships, I've also designed posters, brochures and newsletters.

Social Media

At POLITICO Europe, I wrote a good deal of the content for the Twitter and Facebook pages. Along the way, I also handled updates on the POLITICO Twitter page of events and EU summits. Every week, I made several short audiogram videos for Twitter using Adobe Photoshop, Audition and Premiere Pro. These videos featured snippets from the EU Confidential podcast. If you want more examples of my social media work, I wrote most of POLITICO Europe's Twitter and Facebook posts from September-December 2018 and much of the content on the Columbia Missourian's Facebook and Twitter pages during June 2018.
